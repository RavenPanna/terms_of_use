Privacy Policy for Dr. Battery

Last updated: October 7, 2024

At Dr. Battery, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Dr. Battery does not collect any personal information from its users. The App functions locally on your device and does not send any data, including information about your battery status, device performance, or app usage, to external servers.

However, the following types of non-personally identifiable information may be collected for the purpose of improving the App:

  • Device Information: This includes general details about your iPhone's hardware and software, such as battery health, device model, and operating system version.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics about how you interact with the App to help us improve its performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

All information collected is used for the sole purpose of:

  • Monitoring and analyzing app performance.
  • Providing insights to users about their battery health and device performance.
  • Ensuring compatibility with different iPhone models and iOS versions.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to any third party.

3. Data Retention

Since Dr. Battery does not collect or store any personal data, there is no data retention policy for personal information. All data processed by the App remains on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as analytics tools to help improve the functionality of the App. These services may collect and process non-personally identifiable information as described in their own privacy policies.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information processed by the App. Since no personal data is transmitted or stored, your privacy is well-protected.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App, and the updated date will be reflected at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about our practices.
